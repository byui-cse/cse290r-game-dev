Unit 05 - Beta Kickoff


Competencies

  1. A completed game project demonstrating technical, creative, and design skills.
    1. Implement multiple levels or equivalent with clear objectives and progression.
    2. Identify and resolve major gameplay issues through beta testing and debugging.
    3. Optimize game performance to ensure a smooth and cohesive player experience.

Activities

  1. Study or review the learning resources as needed.
  2. Conduct peer feedback sessions focused on level flow and challenge balancing.
  3. Conduct debugging sessions to identify and resolve critical gameplay issues.
  4. Conduct performance optimization sessions (e.g., frame rate) and resolve critical issues.

Deliverables

  1. Initial beta version of the game with:
    • Two or more polished levels or the equivalent showcasing progression through challenges.
    • Major gameplay issues resolved and smoother transitions between scenes.
  2. A short video walkthrough explaining the challenges and solutions.

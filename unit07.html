Unit 07 - Gold Candidate


Competencies

  1. Experience presenting a game in a professional context, such as a contest or demo.
    1. Develop promotional materials to effectively market the game.
    2. Deliver a rehearsed and engaging demo to showcase the game.
    3. Finalize and package the game for public presentation.

Activities

  1. Study or review the learning resources as needed.
  2. Create promotional materials, including a trailer and game screenshots.
  3. Rehearse game presentations and demos with peer and instructor feedback.
  4. Conduct final debugging and testing to ensure the game is ready for delivery.

Deliverables

  1. Gold candidate version of the game with:
    • Game trailer, screenshots, and description.
    • A polished, release-ready game build.
    • A rehearsed and refined presentation/demo prepared for the game contest.
  2. A short video presentation or demonstration of the game.

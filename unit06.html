Unit 06 - Beta Completion


Competencies

  1. A completed game project demonstrating technical, creative, and design skills.
    1. Apply a feature freeze to finalize core gameplay features and mechanics.
    2. Identify and fix remaining bugs and performance issues.
    3. Polish visuals and audio to ensure a cohesive and professional game experience.

Activities

  1. Study or review the learning resources as needed.
  2. Finalize implementation and lock in all core gameplay features.
  3. Conduct detailed play-testing sessions to identify bugs and usability issues.
  4. Enhancing visuals and audio for a polished look and feel.

Deliverables

  1. Final beta version of the game with:
    • All core gameplay features finalized.
    • Polished visuals, audio, and performance.
  2. A short video walkthrough explaining the remaining issues and fixes.

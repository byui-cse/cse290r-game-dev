Unit 04 - Alpha Expansion


Competencies

  1. A completed game project demonstrating technical, creative, and design skills.
    1. Conduct structured play-testing sessions to gather feedback.
    2. Refine game mechanics, levels, and usability based on feedback.
    3. Enhance the game with representative visual and audio assets.

Activities

  1. Study or review the learning resources as needed.
  2. Conduct peer play-testing sessions and gather structured feedback.
  3. Iterate on core mechanics and AI or scripted behaviors based on feedback.
  4. Find or create representative visual and audio elements.
  5. Begin integrating visual and audio elements to enhance immersion.

Deliverables

  1. Revised alpha version of the game with:
    • Adjustments based on peer feedback.
    • At least one fully playable level or equivalent with improved usability.
    • Representative visual and audio assets integrated.
  2. A short video walkthrough explaining the feedback and improvements.
